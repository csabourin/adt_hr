SECTION H – WORKING CONDITIONS / CONDITIONS DE TRAVAIL

Any question relative to the application or interpretation of working condition s has to be addressed with a Labour Relations Advisor/HR.

Note: The working conditions indicated in this section have to be approved, as the case may be, as specified in the:

  • Relevant collective agreement when available
  • Directive on Terms and Conditions of Employment for Executives
  • Directive on Leave and Special Working Arrangements

Directive on Terms and Conditions of Employment for Certain Excluded and Unrepresented Groups and Levels

Toute question relative à l’application ou interprétation des conditions de travail doit être adressée à un conseiller en relations de travail/RH.

Note : Les conditions de travail indiquées dans cette section doivent être approuvées, selon le cas, conformément à la:

  • Directive sur les congés et autres modalités de travail spéciales
