Prepare the Financial Situation Report

Task

1. Follow up on:
  • Travel claims
  • Journal vouchers
  • Outstanding invoices

2. Close:
  • Commitments

3. Reconcile:
  • Acquisition card statement against Commitments

4. Update:
  • Salary Forecast

5. Identify:
  • Shortfalls
  • Surplus
  • Risks
  • Pressures
  • Carry forward amounts

6. Follow procedures to:
  • Seek approval for additional resources
  • Transfer funds between O&M and Salary

7. Explain variances:
  • Salary budget
  • O&M budget

8. Explain delays:
  • Procurement
  • Changes in work
  • Staffing
